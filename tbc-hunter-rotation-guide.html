Melee weaving

The repo treats weaving as a two-axis problem. Use the common proc-state guide instead of only looking at ranged eWS.

Low or simple haste

  • No haste or drums only: French weaving
  • Improved Hawk only: 2:2 1w
  • DST or Bloodlust without extra ranged-only haste: 2:2 1w

Rapid Fire bands

  • Rapid Fire: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus drums: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Hawk: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Hawk plus drums: 6:11:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust: 6:11:1:1 3w

Maximum weave haste

Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust plus Hawk, or anything below eWS 0.94, pushes you into 3:7 2w.

Reference notes

Field rules

  1. Only cast Steady immediately after an auto.
  2. If Steady would clip the next auto, use Multi-Shot or Arcane Shot instead.
  3. BM and SV share the same timing map. BM just enters it farther right.

Naming note

Weaving names on this page follow the README wording, not the stale comments in gear.yaml.
