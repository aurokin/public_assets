TBC Hunter Rotation Field Guide

Use the shot rhythm your haste actually supports.

This page turns the repo’s rotation scans into something you can use mid-raid. The core rule is simple: measure your effective weapon swing, then move to the matching ranged cycle. For weaving, use the common proc-state guide instead of pretending the problem is only one-dimensional.

Fast read

  • BM base sits in French.
  • BM Hawk or DST pushes you into long French.
  • Rapid Fire usually means 1:1 or skipping.
  • Full stack is 2:5 for ranged and 3:7 2w for weaving.

BM Cheat Sheet

What BM actually plays

  • Base BM or drums only: French 5:5:1:1
  • Hawk proc or DST: long French 5:6:1:1
  • Bloodlust only or Rapid Fire only: 1:1
  • Rapid Fire plus Hawk: skipping 5:9:1:1
  • Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust: skipping 5:9:1:1
  • Rapid Fire plus BL plus Hawk: 2:3
  • Full phase-1 stack: 2:5

SV Cheat Sheet

Where SV starts slower

  • Base SV: short French 5:4:1:1
  • DST proc: French 5:5:1:1
  • Bloodlust only: long French 5:6:1:1
  • Rapid Fire only: long French 5:6:1:1
  • Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust: 1:1
  • Full phase-1 stack without Hawk talent: 1:2

Melee Weaving

Use the proc-state guide, not just the ranged eWS.

The repo treats weaving as a two-axis problem because melee and ranged haste do not move together once ranged-only effects come online.

Low or simple haste

  • No haste or drums only: French weaving
  • Improved Hawk only: 2:2 1w
  • DST or Bloodlust without extra ranged-only haste: 2:2 1w

Rapid Fire bands

  • Rapid Fire: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus drums: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Hawk: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Hawk plus drums: 6:11:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust: 6:11:1:1 3w

Maximum weave haste

Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust plus Hawk, or anything below eWS 0.94, pushes you into 3:7 2w.

Core Rule

Only Steady right after an auto.

If a full Steady would clip the next auto, use the shorter reset windows from Multi-Shot or Arcane Shot instead. Most of the complex rotations are just structured ways to do that repeatedly.

Remember

BM and SV share the same timing map.

They mainly differ in where they enter it. BM starts faster because of Serpent’s Swiftness. SV starts slower, so it spends more time in short French and French.
