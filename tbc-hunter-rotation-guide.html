TBC Hunter rotation guide

A practical reference built from the `tbc_rotationtools` repo. Use effective weapon swing for ranged rotation swaps. For weaving, use the common haste-state guide because melee and ranged haste do not move together.

Fast read
BM base is French. Hawk or DST moves BM to long French.
High haste
Rapid Fire usually means 1:1 or skipping before the very fast combined cycles.
Maximum stack
Full phase-1 BM stack is 2:5 ranged and 3:7 2w for weaving.

BM cheat sheet

  • Base BM or drums only: French 5:5:1:1
  • Hawk proc or DST: long French 5:6:1:1
  • Bloodlust only or Rapid Fire only: 1:1
  • Rapid Fire plus Hawk: skipping 5:9:1:1
  • Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust: skipping 5:9:1:1
  • Rapid Fire plus BL plus Hawk: 2:3
  • Full phase-1 stack: 2:5

SV cheat sheet

  • Base SV: short French 5:4:1:1
  • DST proc: French 5:5:1:1
  • Bloodlust only: long French 5:6:1:1
  • Rapid Fire only: long French 5:6:1:1
  • Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust: 1:1
  • Full phase-1 stack without Hawk talent: 1:2

Melee weaving

The repo treats weaving as a two-axis problem. Use the common proc-state guide instead of only looking at ranged eWS.

Low or simple haste

  • No haste or drums only: French weaving
  • Improved Hawk only: 2:2 1w
  • DST or Bloodlust without extra ranged-only haste: 2:2 1w

Rapid Fire bands

  • Rapid Fire: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus drums: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Hawk: 6:9:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Hawk plus drums: 6:11:1:1 3w
  • Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust: 6:11:1:1 3w

Maximum weave haste

Rapid Fire plus Bloodlust plus Hawk, or anything below eWS 0.94, pushes you into 3:7 2w.

Rules to remember

  • Only Steady immediately after an auto.
  • If Steady would clip an auto, use Multi-Shot or Arcane Shot instead.
  • BM and SV follow the same timing map. BM just enters it farther right.
  • Weaving recommendations follow the README naming, not the drifted comments in `gear.yaml`.

How to read total haste

  • BM base with quiver and Serpent’s Swiftness is `1.38`.
  • SV base with quiver only is `1.15`.
  • eWS is `weapon speed / total ranged haste`.
